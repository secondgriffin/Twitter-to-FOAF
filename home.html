twitter2foaf.appspot.com

twitter2foaf.appspot.com is a very simple RDF FOAF provider that takes information from twitter.com and translates it into a FOAF document.

To use, point an RDF capable user-agent at http://twitter2foaf.appspot.com/id/username. For example, information about Biz Stone is available at http://twitter2foaf.appspot.com/id/biz when using an RDF capable user-agent. (Otherwise, this will just redirect you to http://twitter.com/biz).

Note, to get the data as RDF rather than being redirected to Twitter, be sure to use the Accept-Type: application/rdf+xml or Accept-Type: text/n3 HTTP header.
